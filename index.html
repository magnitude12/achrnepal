About Us

ACHRN, which stands for Anti Corruption Human Rights Nepal, is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting transparency, accountability, and human rights in Nepal. The organization was founded with the aim of combating corruption and promoting human rights, especially for marginalized communities in Nepal.

ACHRN works in collaboration with other civil society organizations, government agencies, and international partners to create a corruption-free society and ensure that human rights are protected and upheld for all. The organization conducts research, advocacy, and awareness-raising activities to promote good governance, transparency, and accountability, and to empower citizens to hold public officials accountable.

ACHRN's ultimate goal is to create a society where everyone has access to justice and their fundamental rights are protected.
